Dictee Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

The short version: Dictee processes everything on your device. We collect no data. We have no servers. Your voice recordings and text never leave your iPhone.

What Dictee Does

Dictee is a dictation app that uses on-device machine learning to transcribe speech, rewrite text, and read text aloud. All three AI models run entirely on your iPhone using Apple's MLX framework.

Data Collection

Dictee does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • Voice recordings are processed on-device and deleted immediately after transcription.
  • Transcribed text exists only in the app's memory during your session. It is not saved to disk or sent anywhere.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting is collected.
  • No network connections are made by the app. Dictee works entirely offline.
  • No accounts or sign-ups are required.

Microphone Access

Dictee requests microphone access solely to record your voice for on-device speech recognition. Audio is captured in a temporary file, processed by the on-device AI model, and then the temporary file is deleted. At no point is your audio transmitted over a network.

Third-Party Services

Dictee does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services. The app has no server-side components.

On-Device AI Models

The machine learning models used by Dictee are bundled within the app and run locally on your device's GPU. No model inference is performed remotely. The models are:

  • Qwen3-ASR (speech recognition)
  • Qwen2.5-1.5B-Instruct (text rewriting)
  • Soprano-80M (text-to-speech)

Data Retention

Since Dictee does not collect any data, there is nothing to retain. Temporary audio files are deleted after each transcription. When you close the app, all session data is gone.

Children's Privacy

Dictee does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
