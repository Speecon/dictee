Frequently Asked Questions

Which devices are supported?

Dictee requires an iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, or any later iPhone with at least 8 GB of RAM. The app uses Apple's MLX framework for on-device AI, which requires a powerful GPU. iPad models with M-series chips are also supported.

Does Dictee need an internet connection?

No. All AI processing happens on your device. Dictee works fully offline, including in airplane mode.

Why is the app so large?

Dictee bundles three AI models (~1.7 GB total) so everything runs locally on your device without needing a server. This is a deliberate choice to ensure complete privacy.

Which languages are supported?

Currently English and German for both speech recognition and text rewriting.

The app shows "Dictee requires iPhone 15 Pro or later"

Your device does not have enough RAM or GPU capability for on-device ML inference. Dictee requires the A17 Pro chip or later, found in iPhone 15 Pro and newer models.

The app gets stuck on the loading screen

Model loading can take 10-30 seconds on first launch. If it fails, tap the "Retry" button. If the problem persists, force-quit the app and relaunch it. Make sure you have at least 2 GB of free storage.
